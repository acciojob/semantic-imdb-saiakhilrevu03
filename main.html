Summary

Inception is a mind-bending science fiction thriller that follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who enters people's dreams to steal secrets. Cobb is given a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can perform the impossible task of inception: planting an idea into someone's subconscious without them knowing.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito, each delivering powerful performances that bring the layers of dreams and reality to life.
